Boracay's Crystal Kayak

Crystal Kayak in Boracay is a perfect experience for those who're looking for the best Instagrammable places in Boracay. In other words, a perfect photo opportunity to have while enjoy Kayaking in Boracay. A kayak is a small, narrow watercraft which is typically propelled by means of a double-bladed paddle and a carbon neutral experience to enjoy the Boracay's natural beauty while peacefully paddling on the picturesque water.

Palawan Island Tours

El Nido is also the epicenter of tourism in Palawan and so it can be quite packed at times. It's a bustling backpacker town with numerous lodges and hostels (and small-scale resorts outside the town as well). The bay is often brimming with bangkas, the local catemaran-style boats which is often used for island hopping.
